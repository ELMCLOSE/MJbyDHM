MAH JONG HANDS - Ordinary
(Max Robertson's book , 2007 edition)

ORDINARY
1.ORDINARY.  Made up of 4 pung/kong/chow's and a pair. All tiles must be from one suit.
:: clean; form: PKC's (one chow only)


PURITY
2.PURITY  is an ordinary hand with no winds or dragons.
:: super-clean; form: PKC's (one C)


ALL KONGS
3.ALL KONGS  hand has 4 kongs in one suit with winds and/or dragons and a pair.
:: clean; form: K's


BURIED TREASURE
4.Buried treasure.  Similar to ordinary hand but cannot pick up discards, i.e. the hand is entirely concealed. Also must have more than one chow.
:: clean; form: PKC's (2 c's); no discards allowed


CHINESE ODDS
7.Chinese odds.  4 pungs/kongs and a pair; only odd numbers of one suit
:: super-clean (odds) ; form: PK's


HEAVENLY PARADISE
18.Heavenly paradise.  A pung/kong of each of the dragons plus a pung/kong of the wind of the round when it is the players own wind, a pair of any wind. A bouquet of flowers must also be held.
:: only WD ; Ordinary ; form:PK for each dragon + PK of own wind + pair of W's


LILY OF THE VALLEY
20.Lily of the valley.  A pung/kong of green and white dragons, 2pungs/kongs of green bamboos (2,3,4,6,8) plus a pair of green bamboos. All tiles may be punged from a discard.
:: Greens & Dragons; form: PK(green dragons) + PK(white dragons) + 2PK of greens + pair


ROYAL RUBY
22. Royal ruby hand.  Similar to the jade hand, but this time using the red dragons and red bamboos (1,5,7,9)
:: Red Bamboos & Red Dragons ; Form: PK of red dragons + PKC of red Bams (1,5,7,9)+pair of red Bams


ALL WINDS AND DRAGONS
29.ALL WINDS AND DRAGONS.  Pungs/kongs and a pair of winds and dragons only, no suit tiles.
:: WD ; PK's and pair


THREE GREAT SCHOLARS
30.THREE GREAT SCHOLARS.  Pungs/kongs of all three dragons plus PKC and a pair.
:: clean ; form:PK of each dragon + PKC + pair i.e.:GGG+RRR+WWW + PKC + pair


THE FOUR BLESSINGS
31.THE FOUR BLESSINGS.  Pungs/kongs of each of the four winds plus a pair of anything.
:: clean ; form:PK of each wind + any pair i.e: NEWS+NEWS+NEWS+pair
