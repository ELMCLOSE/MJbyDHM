MAH JONG HANDS - KNITTING
(Max Robertson's book , 2007 edition)

DOUBLE KNITTING
11.Knitting.  7 pairs of two suits only, all but the last from the wall.
:: two suits - no WD ; form: identical 7-digit numbers in two suits. eg: 2445689 (in bamboos) and 2445689 (in circles)


TRIPLE KNITTING
12.Tripple knitting.  Four sets of 3 tiles and a knitted pair.
:: 3 suits -- no WD; form: three identical 4-digit numbers, one in each suit. e.g: 2358 (in bamboos) +2358 (in circles) + 2358 (in characters) + 11 (different suits)
